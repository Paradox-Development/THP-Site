Client's Rights & Royalties

At True Haven Press, your work remains entirely your own. We do not claim or retain any rights to your book — you retain full ownership of your intellectual property. Manuscript Submission

Manuscripts must be submitted as Microsoft Word documents (.doc or .docx). PDFs are accepted but require conversion and formatting fees.

Editing Process

Authors must review editorial work using a laptop or desktop computer with Microsoft Word installed to prevent formatting errors. Our editing team provides professional suggestions following industry standards, but authors maintain final creative control.

All editorial feedback — including suggestions, rewrites, and notes — will be provided using Microsoft Word's Track Changes feature. Training and support for Track Changes are available upon request.

The publisher flags potentially questionable material but assumes no liability for libel, plagiarism, or copyright infringement claims. Responsibility for such matters remains with the author.
